One of the most innovative features of Proton Pass is its integrated identity protection. Every time you sign up for a new service or newsletter, you are asked for your email address. This address often becomes the primary identifier that data brokers use to track you across the web and build a profile to sell to advertisers 10, 11\.  
Proton Pass includes a **hide-my-email alias** feature. This allows you to generate unique, random email addresses for every account you create. If a service starts spamming you or suffers a data breach, you can simply disable that specific alias without compromising your main email address or having to change your credentials everywhere else 13\. Security is not static; it requires constant vigilance. Proton Pass includes **Pass Monitor**, a suite of advanced security tools designed to keep you a step ahead of cybercriminals.
